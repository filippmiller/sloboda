## 1. PROBLEM STATEMENT

### Market Problem

1. **AI Displacement**: Knowledge workers face mass unemployment within 2-5 years
2. **Urban Lock-in**: City dwellers have no affordable exit strategy
3. **Individual Impossibility**: Single person cannot:
   - Find appropriate rural land
   - Verify legal status
   - Build infrastructure
   - Manage remotely
   - Create sustainable community

### Target Pain Points

| Pain | Intensity |
|------|-----------|
| Fear of job loss to AI | High |
| Fear of losing everything | High |
| Inability to act alone | High |
| Distrust of traditional investments | Medium |
| Desire for tangible assets | High |

---

## 2. SOLUTION

### Core Offering

Managed participation in rural land infrastructure with:
- Multiple entry modes (collective, tokenized, direct ownership)
- Professional management
- Built-in exit mechanisms
- Geographic flexibility (multi-country)

### What Participants Get

| Immediate | Medium-term | Long-term |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Legal rights to property access | Infrastructure development | Full-service living option |
| Ownership certificate/token | Rental income potential | Geographic mobility |
| Community membership | Asset appreciation potential | "Plan B" security |

## 5. PRODUCT TIERS

### Tier A: Collective Share

**Price**: ~$30,000 (2.5M RUB)
**Gets**:
- Share in collective ownership
- Usage rights to assigned property
- Rental income share
- Infrastructure access
- Community membership
- Exit at NAV formula

**Target**: Majority of participants

### Tier B: Tokenized Share

**Price**: ~$30,000 + token issuance fee
**Gets**:
- Everything in Tier A
- Plus: Digital token representing share
- Internal marketplace trading
- Easier transfer/inheritance

**Target**: Tech-savvy, liquidity-conscious

### Tier C: Direct Ownership

**Price**: ~$40,000-$50,000 (premium)
**Gets**:
- Land in personal name (registry)
- Mandatory management contract (10-25 yr)
- Must follow cluster standards
- Higher ongoing management fees

**Target**: High-trust-requirement, family planning
