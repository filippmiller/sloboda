## Executive Summary

This research provides comprehensive, current (2026) requirements for registering an Автономная некоммерческая организация (АНО) in Russia. Key findings:

- **Single founder allowed**: АНО can be created by one physical or legal person
- **Registration authority**: Ministry of Justice (Минюст), not налоговая
- **Registration period**: ~1.5 months
- **State fee**: 4,000 rubles (waived for electronic submission via Госуслуги)
- **Critical 2026 changes**: No paper certificates issued from March 1, 2026; electronic notifications only
- **New portal requirement**: All NGOs must register on nco.minjust.gov.ru and upload charter within 30 days

## 1. Legal Framework

### 1.1 Federal Law ФЗ-7 "On Non-Commercial Organizations"

**Article 10** defines АНО as:

> "Автономной некоммерческой организацией признается не имеющая членства некоммерческая организация, учрежденная гражданами и (или) юридическими лицами на основе добровольных имущественных взносов в целях предоставления услуг в области образования, здравоохранения, культуры, науки, права, физической культуры и спорта и иных услуг."

**Key provisions from ФЗ-7, Article 10**:

- АНО is a **membership-free** organization
- Can be founded by **one or more** citizens and/or legal entities
- Based on **voluntary property contributions**
- Purpose: Providing services in education, healthcare, culture, science, law, physical culture, sports, and other services
- **Property transferred by founders becomes АНО property** (founders have no rights to it)
- **Founders are not liable** for АНО obligations; АНО is not liable for founder obligations
- Founders may use АНО services **only on equal terms** with other persons

**Latest amendments**: Law updated with changes effective from March 1, 2026.

**Source**: [Федеральный закон "О некоммерческих организациях" от 12.01.1996 N 7-ФЗ - КонсультантПлюс](https://www.consultant.ru/document/cons_doc_LAW_8824/) | [Статья 10 - КонсультантПлюс](https://www.consultant.ru/document/cons_doc_LAW_8824/c7d4313ea76843ee327f699ed692067c62c65d72/)

### 1.2 Civil Code of the Russian Federation (ГК РФ) - Articles 123.24-123.25

#### Article 123.24 - Basic Provisions on АНО

**Full requirements**:

1. **Definition**: АНО is a legal entity without membership that provides for pooling of property contributions to achieve statutory goals.

2. **Number of founders**: АНО **can be created by one person** (can have one founder).

3. **Charter must contain**:
   - Organization name (including words "автономная некоммерческая организация")
   - Location (место нахождения)
   - Subject and objectives of activities (предмет и цели деятельности)
   - Composition, procedure for formation, and authority of governing bodies (состав, порядок образования и компетенция органов)
   - Other legally required information

4. **Property rights**:
   - Property transferred to АНО by founders becomes **АНО's property**
   - Founders **do not retain rights** to property transferred to АНО

5. **Liability**:
   - Founders do **not bear liability** for АНО obligations
   - АНО does **not bear liability** for founder obligations

6. **Service usage**:
   - Founders may use АНО services **only on equal terms** with other persons

**Source**: [ГК РФ Статья 123.24 - КонсультантПлюс](https://www.consultant.ru/document/cons_doc_LAW_5142/2d5c3e7ade277916ba566670dcab3450bc8dfde1/) | [Ст. 123.24 ГК РФ - Rulaws.ru](https://rulaws.ru/gk-rf-chast-1/Razdel-I/Glava-4/paragraph-7/Statya-123.24/)

#### Article 123.25 - Management of АНО

**Governance structure**:

1. **Management by founders**:
   - Management is carried out by founders in the manner established by the charter
   - Charter is approved by founders

2. **Collegial bodies (optional)**:
   - By decision of founders, a **permanent collegial body** (bodies) may be created
   - Competence is established by charter

3. **Single executive body (mandatory)**:
   - Founders **must appoint** a single executive body (chairman, general director, etc.)
   - This executive body **may be one of the founders** who is a citizen

**Key insight**: A single founder can simultaneously be the director.

**Source**: [ГК РФ Статья 123.25 - КонсультантПлюс](https://www.consultant.ru/document/cons_doc_LAW_5142/06a2b87c4d3ae4df8a9341a8826a50a7869f0fc6/) | [Ст. 123.25 ГК РФ - Rulaws.ru](https://rulaws.ru/gk-rf-chast-1/Razdel-I/Glava-4/paragraph-7/Statya-123.25/)

## 2. Charter (Устав) Requirements

### 2.1 Mandatory Charter Provisions

According to **ГК РФ 123.24** and **ФЗ-7 Article 10**, the charter must contain:

1. **Organization name**:
   - Must include words "автономная некоммерческая организация"
   - Must indicate character of activities
   - Must not conflict with morality or religious feelings
   - Must not duplicate existing registered organizations

2. **Location** (место нахождения):
   - Legal address

3. **Subject and objectives** (предмет и цели деятельности):
   - **CRITICAL**: Must be an **exhaustive (closed) list** ("закрытый перечень")
   - Cannot include phrases like "иная деятельность, соответствующая законодательству РФ"
   - АНО cannot expand activities beyond what's fixed in charter
   - Must correspond to ОКВЭД codes

4. **Governance structure** (состав, порядок образования и компетенция органов):
   - Composition and competence of АНО bodies
   - Decision-making procedures
   - Matters requiring unanimous or qualified majority voting

5. **Property rights and distribution**:
   - Property rights and obligations of members (if applicable)
   - Procedure for distributing property after liquidation

6. **For educational АНО** (if applicable):
   - Type of education the organization implements

**Source**: [Разработка Устава в АНО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/sub/Ustav_ANO) | [Уставные цели НКО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/article/Ustavnye_celi_NKO)

### 2.2 "Закрытый перечень" (Closed List) Requirement

**Critical Минюст requirement**:

The charter must contain an **exhaustive (closed) list** of goals and activities. This means:

- ✅ **Allowed**: "Организация занимается: [specific activity 1], [specific activity 2], [specific activity 3]"
- ❌ **Prohibited**: "Организация занимается вышеуказанными видами деятельности, а также иной деятельностью, не запрещенной законодательством РФ"

**Consequences of violation**:

- Registration denial by Минюст
- If discovered post-registration: Activity suspension until corrections made
- Possible forced liquidation through court

**Recommendation**: Use Минюст's model charters as templates.

**Source**: [Уставные цели НКО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/article/Ustavnye_celi_NKO) | [Типовые уставы - Ural-NKO](https://ural-nko.ru/ustav_nko)

### 2.3 Model Charters (Типовые уставы)

**Official resource**: Ministry of Justice published model charters on June 30, 2023 (Order No. 163).

**Available at**: [Модельный устав НКО - Минюст](https://minjust.ru/ru/node/262463)

**Key features**:

- Optional to use (not mandatory)
- Can be used as-is or adapted
- Significantly reduce rejection risk
- Reflect current Минюст expectations

**Alternative**: Develop custom charter, but ensure compliance with all legal requirements.

**Source**: [Типовые уставы для НКО - КонсультантПлюс](https://www.consultant.ru/legalnews/22769/) | [Модельный устав - Минюст](https://minjust.gov.ru/ru/node/262463)

### 2.4 Technical Requirements for Charter

**Formatting**:

- All charter copy sheets must be **numbered**
- Format: PDF for electronic submission
- Must be uploaded to nco.minjust.gov.ru within 30 days of registration (new 2026 requirement)

**Source**: [Разработка Устава в АНО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/sub/Ustav_ANO)

## 3. Governance Structure Requirements

### 3.1 Highest Governing Body (Высший орган управления)

**Mandatory requirement**: АНО **must have a collegial highest governing body**.

**Key rules**:

1. **Composition**:
   - Must consist of **at least 2 persons** (collegial requirement)
   - Can be formed from founders and/or third parties (as specified in charter)
   - Employees of АНО cannot constitute **more than 1/3** of total members

2. **Powers** (exclusive competence):
   - Determining priority directions of АНО activities
   - Principles of property formation and use
   - Amending and approving charter
   - Determining procedure for admission and exclusion of founders
   - Establishing АНО bodies and early termination of their powers
   - Approving annual reports and financial statements
   - Reorganization and liquidation decisions

**For single founder АНО**:

The highest governing body must still be **collegial** (at least 2 persons). Options:

- Founder + appointed member(s)
- Founder can be part of the collegial body but cannot be the sole member

**Source**: [Структура органов управления АНО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/sub/Struktura_upravleniya_ANO) | [Управление в АНО с единственным учредителем - RegForum](https://regforum.ru/posts/3044_upravlenie_v_ano_s_edinstvennym_uchreditelem/)

### 3.2 Single Executive Body (Единоличный исполнительный орган)

**Mandatory requirement**: Founders must appoint a single executive body.

**Options**:

- Chairman (Председатель)
- General Director (Генеральный директор)
- Director (Директор)
- Other title as specified in charter

**Key rule**: The executive body **can be one of the founders** who is a citizen.

For single founder АНО: The founder can simultaneously be the director.

**Source**: [ГК РФ Статья 123.25](https://www.consultant.ru/document/cons_doc_LAW_5142/06a2b87c4d3ae4df8a9341a8826a50a7869f0fc6/)

### 3.3 Попечительский совет (Supervisory Board)

**Status**: **OPTIONAL** for АНО (not mandatory).

**Key clarifications**:

- **Mandatory only for**: Благотворительные фонды (charitable foundations)
- **Optional for**: АНО and other НКО forms

**If created**:

- Can serve as supervisory body, advisory body, or even highest governing body
- Structure and powers defined by charter and internal regulations
- No legal requirements for candidate qualifications (can be set in charter)
- Size determined by Supervisory Board itself
- Changes to composition require unanimous vote

**Recommendation**: For simple АНО, a Supervisory Board is not necessary. Focus on mandatory bodies: highest governing body (collegial) and single executive body.

**Source**: [Попечительский совет фонда - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/sub/Popechitelskij_sovet_fonda) | [Зачем нужен попечительский совет - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/forum-nko/theme/zachem-nuzhen-popechitelskij-sovet)

## 4. Document Package for Registration

### 4.1 Required Documents

**For АНО registration, submit**:

1. **Application (Заявление)**: Form Р11001
2. **Charter (Устав)**: 3 copies (2 for Минюст, 1 returned with registration stamp)
3. **Decision/Protocol**:
   - Single founder: "Решение единственного учредителя о создании АНО"
   - Multiple founders: "Протокол общего собрания учредителей АНО"
4. **Proof of state fee payment**: 4,000 rubles (not required for electronic submission)
5. **Information about founders**:
   - Physical persons: Full name, passport data, registration address
   - Legal persons: Company details, registration documents
6. **Passport copies**: For all founders (physical persons)

**Source**: [Регистрация АНО в 2026 году - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/div/Avtonomnye_NKO) | [Документы для регистрации НКО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/sub/Dokumenty_dlya_registracii_NKO)

### 4.2 Form Р11001 - Application

**Official form**: Approved by Order of the Federal Tax Service dated August 31, 2020 № ЕД-7-14/617@

**Current status**: This form remains valid for 2026 registrations.

**CRITICAL**: Using outdated forms will result in rejection. Always download from official FTS website or verified sources.

**Sheets to complete for АНО**:

- Title page
- Sheets with information about founders
- Information about director (or other person performing functions)
- Information about applicant

**Formatting requirements**:

- Printed capital letters of Russian alphabet
- Manual filling: Black, blue, or violet ink
- Computer preparation: Only Courier New font, 18 points, black color
- Each cell contains only one character

**Source**: [Форма Р11001 для НКО 2026 - Regberry](https://www.regberry.ru/registraciya-ooo/obrazcy-dokumentov/forma-r11001-zapolnenie) | [Регистрация НКО в 2026 году - Dvitex](https://www.dvitex.ru/poleznoe/biznes/registratsiya-kompaniy/registratsiya-nekommercheskoy-organizatsii-poshagovaya-instruktsiya-i-sroki/)

### 4.3 Decision of Single Founder (Решение единственного учредителя)

**When required**: If АНО is founded by one person.

**Must contain**:

1. **Header**:
   - "Решение единственного учредителя автономной некоммерческой организации [NAME]"
   - Date and place of signing

2. **Resolved issues** (mandatory):
   - Creation of АНО
   - Approval of charter
   - Election/appointment of director (single executive body)
   - Determination of АНО location
   - Appointment of person authorized to act on behalf of АНО during registration
   - Formation of highest governing body (if applicable)

3. **Signature**: Founder's signature

**Source**: [Решение единственного учредителя АНО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/documents/sozdanieANO.pdf) | [Решение единственного учредителя АНО - URFlex](https://urflex.ru/reshenie-edinstvennogo-uchreditelya-ano-pri-ego-sozdanii.html)

### 4.4 Electronic Submission via Госуслуги

**Portal**: gosuslugi.ru

**Requirements**:

1. **Verified account** on Госуслуги (confirmed identity)
2. **Qualified electronic signature** (усиленная квалифицированная электронная подпись - ЭЦП)
3. **Documents in PDF format**
4. **All regional protocols must be archived** (if АНО has structural subdivisions)

**Benefits of electronic submission**:

- ✅ **No state fee** (4,000 rubles saved)
- ✅ No notary expenses
- ✅ Faster processing
- ✅ Remote submission

**Drawback**:

- Requires obtaining qualified electronic signature (cost and time)

**Source**: [Регистрация НКО через Госуслуги - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/article/Registraciya_NKO_onlajn) | [Документы для регистрации НКО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/sub/Dokumenty_dlya_registracii_NKO)

## 5. ОКВЭД Codes for АНО

### 5.1 General Requirements

**What is ОКВЭД**: Общероссийский классификатор видов экономической деятельности (All-Russian Classifier of Economic Activities)

**Current version**: ОКВЭД-2 (updated January 13, 2026)

**Requirements for АНО**:

- ОКВЭД codes are **mandatory** for registration
- Codes must **correspond to charter activities**
- Mismatch between ОКВЭД and charter = **registration denial**
- As of March 2023, НКО can use **specialized НКО codes**

**Source**: [ОКВЭД 2026 - Regberry](https://www.regberry.ru/malyy-biznes/okved) | [Новые коды ОКВЭД для НКО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/smi/okved-dlya-nko)

### 5.2 Recommended ОКВЭД Codes for Rural Development & Eco-Settlements

Based on SLOBODA project goals (rural development, eco-settlements, sustainable living), recommended codes:

#### Primary Activities:

1. **94.99** - Деятельность прочих общественных организаций, не включенных в другие группировки
   - **Primary НКО code** - can be used alone without further specification
   - Voluntary to add specific codes
   - **Recommended as main code**

2. **88.99** - Предоставление прочих социальных услуг без обеспечения проживания, не включенных в другие группировки
   - Social services provision
   - Community support activities

#### Rural Development & Agriculture:

3. **01.70** - Охота, отлов и отстрел диких животных, включая предоставление услуг в этих областях
   - Hunting and wildlife services
   - Note: Likely not suitable for SLOBODA unless hunting services planned

4. **01.6x** - Деятельность вспомогательная в области производства сельскохозяйственных культур и послеуборочной обработки сельхозпродукции
   - Support activities for crop production
   - **Recommended for agricultural support services**

#### Tourism & Accommodation (if eco-tourism/guest houses planned):

5. **55.xx** - Деятельность по предоставлению мест для временного проживания
   - Temporary accommodation provision
   - **Recommended if eco-village guest houses/tourism planned**

6. **79.11** - Деятельность туристических агентств
   - Travel agency activities
   - **Recommended if organizing eco-tours**

7. **79.12** - Деятельность туроператоров
   - Tour operator activities

#### Education (if educational programs on sustainability/ecology):

8. **85.59** - Дополнительное образование, не включенное в другие группировки
   - Additional education not elsewhere classified
   - **Recommended for workshops, courses on sustainable living**

#### Environmental/Ecological Activities:

9. **91.33** - Деятельность экологических и природоохранных движений
   - **NEW code for НКО** (available since March 2023)
   - Environmental and nature protection movements
   - **HIGHLY RECOMMENDED for eco-settlement focus**

**Source**: [ОКВЭД раздел A Сельское хозяйство - Regfile](https://www.regfile.ru/okved2/razdel-a.html) | [ОКВЭД 79 Туризм - Regfile](https://www.regfile.ru/okved2/razdel-n/79.html) | [ОКВЭДы НКО: изменения - ASI](https://asi.org.ru/2023/04/25/okvedy/)

### 5.3 ОКВЭД Selection Strategy for SLOBODA АНО

**Recommended primary code**: **94.99** (general НКО activities)

**Recommended additional codes** (based on charter activities):

- **91.33** - Environmental/ecological movements (core mission)
- **88.99** - Social services provision (community support)
- **85.59** - Additional education (sustainability workshops)
- **55.xx** - Temporary accommodation (if eco-tourism/guest houses)

**CRITICAL**: Ensure all ОКВЭД codes are reflected in charter's "закрытый перечень" of activities.

## 6. Common Rejection Reasons by Минюст

### 6.1 Most Common Reasons (2025-2026)

Based on current Минюст practice:

1. **Name issues**:
   - Name does not correspond to activities
   - Name duplicates existing registered organization
   - Name conflicts with morality or religious feelings
   - Foreign language in name (prohibited)

2. **Charter non-compliance**:
   - Charter provisions contradict ФЗ-7 or ГК РФ
   - Governance structure does not comply with НКО legislation
   - Missing mandatory charter provisions
   - Open-ended activities list ("иная деятельность...")

3. **Document errors**:
   - Incorrect form Р11001 (outdated version)
   - Typos, errors, inconsistencies
   - Unnumbered charter pages
   - Protocol errors (wrong format, missing signatures)

4. **Jurisdictional errors**:
   - Documents submitted to wrong Минюст territorial office
   - Founder lacks right to create НКО

5. **ОКВЭД-charter mismatch**:
   - ОКВЭД codes don't correspond to charter activities
   - Charter activities not covered by ОКВЭД codes

**Source**: [Основные причины отказа в регистрации НКО - Jus Liberum](https://jusliberum.ru/registraciya-nko/otkaz-v-registracii-nko) | [Отказы Минюста - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/article/Otkazy_Minusta_v_gosudarstvennoy_registracii_NKO)

### 6.2 New Rejection Grounds (Effective February 5, 2025)

**Federal Law 129-ФЗ amendments**: Two new grounds for rejection added.

**Details**: Not fully specified in search results, but indicates tightening of requirements.

**Source**: [Новые основания для отказа - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/smi/novye-osnovaniya-dlya-otkaza-v-gosudarstvennoj-registracii)

### 6.3 Regional Practice Variations

**CRITICAL ISSUE**: Минюст territorial offices apply requirements inconsistently.

**Example**: Charter registered in Moscow may be rejected in another region due to different interpretations.

**Implication**: Engage legal professionals familiar with **local Минюст practice** in your registration region.

**Source**: [Регистрация НКО: 5 частых ошибок - Dobro.Press](https://dobro.press/lifestyle/registracziya-nko-5-chastyh-oshibok-pri-zapolnenii-dokumentov)

### 6.4 Common Charter Mistakes

**Specific errors to avoid**:

1. **Incomplete regulatory basis**:
   - Governance competence often spread across multiple laws
   - Citing only one law = rejection risk

2. **Missing paid services provision**:
   - НКО can provide paid services, but many forget to include this
   - Omission = cannot provide paid services later without charter amendment

3. **Non-compliance with sector-specific regulations**:
   - Charter must account for legislation regulating specific activity spheres
   - Example: Educational АНО must comply with education law requirements

4. **Foreign language usage**:
   - Any foreign language in official documents = automatic rejection

**Source**: [Документы для регистрации НКО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/sub/Dokumenty_dlya_registracii_NKO) | [Регистрация НКО в Минюсте - Pravila Registratsii](https://xn--80abfedtavrkbe1aq0c.xn--p1ai/blog/pravila-registracii-nko)

## 7. Registration Process & Timelines

### 7.1 Registration Timeline (2026)

**Total period**: Approximately **1.5 months** (45 days)

**Breakdown**:

1. **Document preparation**: 1-2 weeks (depending on complexity)
2. **Минюст review**: ~30 days (statutory period)
3. **FTS registration**: ~5 business days
4. **Document retrieval**: 1-2 days

**Source**: [Регистрация АНО в 2026 году - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/div/Avtonomnye_NKO) | [Регистрация НКО в 2026 году - Dvitex](https://www.dvitex.ru/poleznoe/biznes/registratsiya-kompaniy/registratsiya-nekommercheskoy-organizatsii-poshagovaya-instruktsiya-i-sroki/)

### 7.2 Registration Authority

**Primary authority**: Ministry of Justice (Минюст) territorial office

**NOT налоговая инспекция** (unlike ООО registration)

**Process**:

1. Минюст reviews and approves documents
2. Минюст forwards to FTS (Налоговая) for ЕГРЮЛ registration
3. FTS assigns ИНН, ОГРН
4. Information returned to Минюст
5. Минюст issues registration confirmation

**Source**: [Регистрация АНО - Dvitex](https://www.dvitex.ru/poleznoe/biznes/registratsiya-kompaniy/registratsiya-avtonomnoy-nekommercheskoy-organizatsii-ano-poshagovaya-instruktsiya-i-sro/)

### 7.3 Critical 2026 Changes

#### Change 1: No Paper Certificates (Effective March 1, 2026)

**Old system**: Минюст issued paper "Свидетельство о государственной регистрации"

**New system (from March 1, 2026)**:
- **No paper certificates**
- **Electronic notifications** only
- Registration confirmed by entry in ЕГРЮЛ
- Electronic notification sent to applicant

**Source**: [Регистрация НКО 2026: новые правила без свидетельств - Nulevoy Balans](https://nulevoybalans.ru/novosti/registratsiya-nko-2026-novye-pravila-bez-svidetelstv.html)

#### Change 2: Mandatory Portal Registration (Effective January 1, 2025)

**New requirement**: All НКО must register on **nco.minjust.gov.ru**

**Obligations**:

1. **Create account** on nco.minjust.gov.ru (requires Госуслуги account linked to АНО)
2. **Upload charter** within 30 days of state registration (PDF format)
3. **Submit annual reports** via portal (deadline: April 15 annually)

**Old portal**: unro.minjust.ru (no longer functional, redirects to new portal)

**Source**: [Портал Минюста для НКО - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/smi/portal-minyusta-dlya-nko-instrukciya-dlya-sotrudnikov-nekommercheskih-organizacij) | [Государственная регистрация НКО - Minjust.gov.ru](https://minjust.gov.ru/ru/activity/govservices/5/)

#### Change 3: New Reporting Form (Effective 2026)

**Previous system**: Multiple report forms to various agencies

**New system (from 2026)**:
- **Single electronic form** submitted to Минюст
- Includes all required statistical information
- Submitted via nco.minjust.gov.ru portal

**Legal basis**: Federal Law № 313-ФЗ (adopted July 31, 2025)

**Source**: [С 2026 года НКО переходят к новой форме отчётности - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/smi/s-2026-goda-nko-perehodyat-k-novoj-forme-otchetnosti)

### 7.4 State Fee

**Amount**: 4,000 rubles

**Payment timing**: Before document submission

**Exemption**: **No fee required** for electronic submission via Госуслуги

**Recommendation**: Use electronic submission to save 4,000 rubles.

**Source**: [Регистрация АНО в 2026 году - Reg-NKO](https://reg-nko.ru/div/Avtonomnye_NKO)

## 8. Post-Registration Requirements (2026)

### 8.1 Immediate Actions After Registration

1. **Register on nco.minjust.gov.ru** (within 30 days):
   - Create organizational account
   - Link Госуслуги employee account to АНО
   - Upload charter (PDF format)

2. **Open bank account**:
   - Use registration documents
   - Required for financial operations

3. **Order organizational seal** (optional but recommended):
   - Not mandatory by law, but useful for documents

4. **Register with Pension Fund and Social Insurance Fund**:
   - Automatic in most cases after ЕГРЮЛ registration
   - Verify registration status

**Source**: [Государственная регистрация НКО - Minjust.gov.ru](https://minjust.gov.ru/ru/activity/govservices/5/)

### 8.2 Annual Reporting Obligations

**Deadline**: **April 15** of each year

**Reports required** (via nco.minjust.gov.ru):

1. Annual report on АНО activities
2. Financial statements
3. Information about personnel
4. Information about founders/members (if changed)

**New form**: Single electronic form (effective 2026)

**Penalties for non-compliance**: Fines, possible forced liquidation

**Action required**: Submit notification to Роскомнадзор after АНО registration

## 9. Practical Recommendations

### 9.1 Preparation Strategy

**Recommended approach**:

1. **Use model charter as template** (download from minjust.ru/ru/node/262463)
2. **Adapt to SLOBODA activities** (ensure exhaustive list of activities)
3. **Engage local legal counsel** familiar with regional Минюст practice
4. **Cross-check ОКВЭД codes** with charter activities (ensure perfect alignment)
5. **Use electronic submission** via Госуслуги (saves 4,000 rubles, faster processing)

### 9.2 Single Founder АНО Specifics

**For SLOBODA (if single founder)**:

1. **Founder**: Physical person (you)
2. **Highest governing body**: Must be collegial (≥2 persons)
   - Option A: You + 1-2 trusted advisors
   - Option B: You + co-founder(s) from community
3. **Director**: Can be you (founder can simultaneously serve as director)

**Document**: "Решение единственного учредителя о создании АНО SLOBODA"

### 9.3 ОКВЭД Selection for SLOBODA

**Recommended codes**:

- **94.99** (primary) - General НКО activities
- **91.33** - Environmental movements (core mission)
- **88.99** - Social services (community support)
- **85.59** - Additional education (sustainability workshops)
- **55.xx** - Accommodation (if eco-village guest services)
- **79.11** - Tourism (if eco-tours organized)

**Ensure charter explicitly lists all activities corresponding to these codes.**

### 9.4 Charter Goals for SLOBODA

**Example exhaustive list** (adapt as needed):

1. Содействие устойчивому развитию сельских территорий
2. Развитие экопоселений и экологически ответственного образа жизни
3. Организация образовательных программ в области устойчивого развития, экологии и сельского хозяйства
4. Содействие сохранению окружающей среды и природных ресурсов
5. Организация культурно-просветительских мероприятий
6. Оказание социальных услуг населению
7. Развитие экологического туризма
8. Предоставление мест для временного проживания в рамках экотуристической деятельности

**CRITICAL**: Do NOT add "и иная деятельность, не запрещенная законом" - this will cause rejection.

### 9.5 Timeline Planning

**Realistic timeline for SLOBODA АНО registration**:

- **Week 1-2**: Document preparation (charter, decision, founder info)
- **Week 3**: Obtain qualified electronic signature (if using Госуслуги)
- **Week 4**: Submit documents electronically via Госуслуги
- **Week 5-9**: Минюст review (up to 30 days)
- **Week 10**: FTS registration (5 business days)
- **Week 11**: Receive electronic notification, register on nco.minjust.gov.ru

**Total**: ~11 weeks (2.5 months) including preparation

### 9.6 Cost Estimates

**Minimum costs** (DIY approach with electronic submission):

- State fee: **0 rubles** (waived for Госуслуги submission)
- Qualified electronic signature: **~1,500-3,000 rubles**
- Notary services (founder documents): **~1,000-2,000 rubles**
- **Total minimum**: **~2,500-5,000 rubles**

**With legal assistance**:

- Legal services (charter preparation, submission): **15,000-50,000 rubles**
- Total with legal help: **~17,500-55,000 rubles**

**Recommendation**: For first-time АНО registration, legal assistance is highly recommended to avoid rejections.
